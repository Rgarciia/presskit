He began to enter the electronic field in 2014, at the age of 19, experimenting with styles such as Trance, Progressive trance, PsyTrance, he has always defined his style for a few years as a DJ.

Later, after having experimented for years with the aforementioned styles, he decided to experiment with a softer and more subtle rhythm (Progressive).

Currently following the line for the diferents sound , he provides an unmatched quality and sensation on the dance floor, filling us with harmony and a lot of melody with his sessions that captivate you from the first track!

Space Venture is the first DJ nickname or alias that allows ReNe Garcïa to explore different styles and approaches in music as part of her identity or stage name.
